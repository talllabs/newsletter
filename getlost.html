June 2026 Issue

The Get Lost Gazette pares things down this month, highlighting only the most compelling headlines from around the world—places that spark curiosity and invite adventurous spirits. Everything here was published within the last 30 days.

Urbanites

White Elephant Aspen – Colorado, USA

Opening 15 June, White Elephant Aspen brings 54 art-filled rooms and suites to Aspen’s West Main Street. Interiors mix mountain materials with a 125-piece art collection; amenities include a ski lounge, pet concierge, outdoor pool and LoLa 41° restaurant.

Romègas Hotel – Valletta, Malta

In Malta’s capital, the 23-room Romègas Hotel revives a 500-year-old palazzo. The boutique property pairs historic stonework with modern design, includes a destination restaurant and a rooftop pool overlooking Valletta’s narrow streets.

Action

Ord Valley Muster – Western Australia

The Kimberley’s Ord Valley Muster returned from 15 to 24 May for its 25th edition, blending world-class music with Aboriginal storytelling and outback experiences. Highlights included the Kimberley Moon Experience, Comedy in the Park and the Corroboree Under the Stars.

New Airline Routes

Long-haul travel got more interesting: AirAsia X relaunched daily Kuala Lumpur–London Gatwick flights via Bahrain on 26 June, its first U.K. route since 2012. EVA Air introduced Taipei–Washington D.C. service and SAS launched the first nonstop link between Scandinavia and India with a Copenhagen–Mumbai route.

Luxe

The Sanctuary Spa – Naples, Florida

At the Four Seasons’ Naples Beach Club, a new Sanctuary Spa opened on 28 May. Inspired by the indigenous Calusa people, the 2,800 m² spa features 13 treatment rooms, aromatherapy steamrooms, a Finnish sauna, ice room, vitality pool and a 23-metre rooftop lap pool.

Ocean Rebirth Retreat – Transatlantic Crossing

Orient Express and Guerlain will host a 14-day Ocean Rebirth retreat aboard the 220-metre sailing yacht Orient Express Corinthian during a Lisbon–Bridgetown crossing (12–26 Oct 2026). Announced on 22 May, the programme will include treatments with Guerlain’s LongeviSkin devices and expert-led sessions on nutrition, movement, mindfulness and sleep.

Into the Wild

Auko Eco-Wellness Lodges – Phong Nha–Ke Bang, Vietnam

Due to open in Q3 2026, Auko is a low-impact eco-wellness retreat near the Son Doong cave. The development will comprise 30 tented lodges along the Son River and three wellness pathways—Still Way, Wild Way and Open Way—offering experiences from mud caves and forest showers to yoga in a tented sky studio.

Full story →

Wilderness Mara – Mara Triangle, Kenya

Wilderness Safaris’ new Wilderness Mara camp took over the former Little Governors’ site this June. Its 12 suites come with a pool, gym and in-room yoga mats; guests dine under the stars in a traditional Manyatta and enjoy menus spotlighting Kenyan produce and Maasai honey.

June’s viral travel soundtrack is Saxboy Billy’s “Puerto Rico Song.” Creators are lip-syncing the catchy hook over clips of city adventures, outfit reveals and day-in-the-life montages.
