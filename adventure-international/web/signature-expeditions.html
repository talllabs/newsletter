Mt Kilimanjaro

Lemosho Route · 8 Days · VIP Specification

The Lemosho Route is regarded as one of the finest journeys on the mountain, celebrated for its scenery, acclimatisation profile, and remote approach. While its popularity has grown, our route management and mountain strategy help guests avoid the crowds and experience quieter, more exclusive terrain.

Our Kilimanjaro teams have worked together for nearly two decades. This mountain is where Adventure International was born, and it remains our speciality. Through training, welfare, education, and community investment, we deliver safe, sustainable, and ethical climbs that set standards on and off the mountain.

As a founding member of KPAP — https://www.kiliporters.org — we proudly champion porter welfare and responsible tourism. Simply put, we are the A-Team for climbing Kilimanjaro.

Himalayan Expeditions

Everest Base Camp & Lobuche East

Beyond the Trek · Real Climbing Experience

For many years we have operated in the high Himalaya, bringing the same standards of welfare, training, sustainability, and elite guiding that define our Kilimanjaro operations.

Our Kathmandu outfitting base and trusted Nepali team allow us to run seamless, high-touch expeditions with exceptional guides, equipment, logistics, and local expertise.

While Everest Base Camp is a lifelong dream for many, we go further. This journey includes Lobuche East Peak, giving guests the chance to develop technical climbing skills in a controlled environment before standing above the Khumbu Valley at sunrise with views of Everest and Ama Dablam.

We travel through Gokyo and Cho La Pass, staying in family guesthouses and independent lodges we have supported for years — ensuring our small groups directly benefit the communities we visit.
