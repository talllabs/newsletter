Luxsurf  —  2026 Itineraries

Surf Beyond the Break

Four signature journeys that fuse world-class waves with wildlife, altitude, and culture — each one a masterclass in adventure travel.

South Africa  ·  Mozambique

African Surfari

Using specially adapted 4×4 vehicles and expert guides, venture to remote beaches and wild surf on the South African and Mozambique border. A private villa at Ponta do Ouro is your base for uncrowded waves, before heading inland on safari — luxury bush camps, the Big Five, and a private game reserve in KwaZulu-Natal’s celebrated Phinda. A true African Surfari.

View Sample Itinerary
 

Sri Lanka  ·  Maldives

Surf & Safari: Sri Lanka & Maldives

A journey of perfect contrasts — from tracking leopards in dense jungle to riding consistent Indian Ocean swells. Sri Lanka’s celebrated “surf and turf” delivers wildlife parks a short drive from world-class breaks. A brief flight then carries you to the serene, uncrowded waves of the Maldives, where luxury and solitude converge in equal measure.

View Sample Itinerary
 

Morocco

Summit to Sea

Swap hiking boots for a wetsuit on the ultimate Moroccan contrast. Luxuriate in the riads of Marrakech, trek deep into the High Atlas Mountains through Berber villages, and summit Mt Toubkal — North Africa’s highest peak. Then descend to the Atlantic coast and the peeling right-handers of Taghazout. High-altitude challenge meets coastal ease, with world-class waves, stunning scenery, and delicious tagines throughout.

View Sample Itinerary
 

Peru

Surfing & Machu Picchu

From the Pacific swells of Lima to the ancient stone paths of the Andes — a rare journey spanning two worlds. Begin in Lima, an underrated surf destination and birthplace of ceviche, before flying to Cusco and ascending into high-altitude passes, glacial lakes, and cloud forest. Luxury glamping, the Sacred Valley, and finally the Sun Gate trail to Machu Picchu itself. Surf and summit, perfectly composed.
